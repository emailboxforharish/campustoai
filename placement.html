What We Do

Your Complete Placement Support

We handle everything between finishing the programme and getting your offer letter.

01

Resume & LinkedIn Makeover

A generic college resume won't get you calls. We rebuild your resume from scratch — highlighting your real projects, tech stack, and GitHub profile. Your LinkedIn gets optimised with keywords recruiters search for.

  • ATS-optimised resume format
  • Project descriptions that impress interviewers
  • LinkedIn headline + summary written for Java/cloud roles
  • Skills section aligned to what companies actually look for
02

GitHub Portfolio Review

Recruiters and hiring managers check GitHub before interviews. We ensure your repositories are clean, documented, and impressive — with proper READMEs, commit history, and live deployment links.

  • Full-stack project with Spring Boot + React + Azure deployment
  • AI-integrated feature that stands out from other freshers
  • Professional README files for every project
  • Clean commit history and branching strategy
03

Mock Technical Interviews

The biggest reason freshers fail interviews is not skill — it's lack of practice. We run multiple full mock interview rounds covering exactly what companies ask Java developers.

  • Core Java + OOP + Collections deep dive
  • Spring Boot REST API scenarios
  • SQL query writing under pressure
  • System design basics for freshers
  • Docker / Kubernetes / Azure conceptual rounds
04

HR & Behavioural Interview Prep

Technical skills alone don't get the offer. We prepare you for HR rounds — salary negotiation, common behavioural questions, and how to present your story as a fresher confidently.

  • "Tell me about yourself" crafted to impress
  • STAR method answers for behavioural questions
  • Salary expectation negotiation guidance
  • Body language and communication tips for video interviews
05

Job Referrals & Direct Connections

We actively connect graduates with our hiring network. Your profile goes directly to hiring managers — not into an ATS black hole. We also post your profile on our company WhatsApp channels for referrals.

  • Direct introductions to hiring managers in our network
  • Profile shared across startup communities & job boards
  • Referral from mentors who are active industry professionals
  • Support through offer negotiation and joining
